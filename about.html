GETTING ADAPTED TO A NEW EDUCATIONAL ENVIRONMENT.

A person who enters into the tertiary education system face many difficulties in getting adapted to a new educational environment. In the secondary education system, they followed their education in their mother’s tongue and also they were able to get their educational resources in their mother’s tongue.

UNABLE TO GET VERIFIED RESOURCES OR GUIDENCE.

Not only this but also they submitted their projects, assignments, other submissions in their mother’s tongue. But in tertiary education, they are unable to get verified resources. Because of this, they face many difficulties.

ACTS AS A MOTIVATOR TO THE FRESHERS.

Unihelpy will serve as a bridge that connects the persons who are in the same interest area. Hence unihelpy acts as a Motivator to the freshers to start their tertiary education easily and in a successful manner.

Why Choose Us

We can avoid the problem faced by freshers by implementing a project like unihelpy. At the same time, This system is very helpful in sharing resources with students within a University or between Universities regarding a certain interesting study area.
